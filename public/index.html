IMDB query app

Routes:

Year

/movies/year/1991

NOTE: This will return results of movies that began on or before the provided year AND finished on or after the provided year.

Genre and filters

/movies/genre/comedy?year=2006

Sorting

/movies/genre/documentary?sort=rating

The following are valid values to sort by:

  • rating
  • genre
  • year
  • title
